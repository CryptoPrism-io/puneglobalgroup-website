## About

**Legal / Trading Name:** Unisource (website domain: unipaper.in)
**Tagline / Brand Line:** "Turn a new page"
**Primary Headline (Homepage):** "Here, innovation comes standard."

**Founded:** 2007
**Founder:** Inder Aurora
**Current Leadership:** Garima Aurora (daughter of founder) — described as co-leader who "drives the business on to new paths while ensuring the company adheres to the values and principles it was founded on."

**Core Mission Statement:** "Trade in the best quality of papers and paperboards, customize them at state-of-the-art facilities, and finish them with trademark after-sales service."

**Business Description (from site):**
The organization stocks everything from just-in-time deliveries and small orders to cutting-edge papers from India's respected mills. Established in 2007, it combines responsible sales practices with responsive after-sales service, positioning itself as the preferred choice for industry leaders.

**Product Range Summary:** 40+ paper and paperboard products spanning food packaging, beverage boards, specialty papers, kraft liners, printing papers, and sustainable alternatives to plastic packaging.

**Strategic Partner:** ITC Papers & Boards Ltd. (partnership since 2014 — contract sheeting operations at Unit 2, Talegaon)
